Biography

Gabriella Torres is a Boston-based multi media artist and Studio Arts major at Bunker Hill Community College. Her work focuses on themes of identity, influenced by her experiences growing up in a Latino household. Her practice continues to develop as she examines the relationship between personal cultural history and visual culture.
